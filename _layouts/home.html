Research

Numerical methods for multiscale computation

We develop efficient and accurate numerical methods to reconstruct multiscale gas/phonon transport phenomena reliably over the whole range of Knudsen numbers. We put forward a general synthetic iterative scheme (GSIS) for steady/transient solutions of kinetic equations, which has merits of fast convergence and asymptotic preserving.
